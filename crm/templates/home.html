Customer Relationship Management

Purpose of this portal is to allow our admins to create new Leads. Allows them to be in touch with leads and see list of all leads and their touches.

How It Works

  • First Admin must register themselves in this portal
  • Login to the portal with their newly created account
  • Now they can create new leads and their touches from navigation links
  • They can also see list of leads and their touches if they exists
